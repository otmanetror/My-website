Frequently Asked Questions

How does the purchase process work?

After payment, you'll receive your Spotify account credentials via email or Telegram within 1-24 hours. You can then login and enjoy premium features immediately.

Are these shared or private accounts?

All accounts are private and for your use only. We do not sell shared accounts that get banned frequently.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept PayPal, cryptocurrency (BTC, ETH), and other payment methods depending on your region. Contact us for details.

What if my account stops working?

All accounts come with a 30-day warranty. If your account stops working within this period, contact us for a replacement.

Can I change the account email and password?

Yes, after receiving the account you can change all details including email and password through Spotify's account settings.
